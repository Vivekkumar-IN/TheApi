Methods

The following methods are available in SaavnAPI:

SaavnAPI.get_album

Retrieves an album by its ID or link.

SaavnAPI.get_artist

Retrieves an artist by their ID.

SaavnAPI.get_artist_albums

Retrieves a list of albums for a given artist by their ID.

SaavnAPI.get_artist_songs

Retrieves a list of songs for a given artist by their ID.

SaavnAPI.get_playlist

Retrieves a playlist by its ID or link.

SaavnAPI.get_song_by_id

Retrieves a song by its ID.

SaavnAPI.get_song_lyrics

Retrieves lyrics for a song by its ID.

SaavnAPI.get_song_suggestions

Retrieves song suggestions based on the given song ID.

SaavnAPI.search

Searches globally for songs, albums, artists, and playlists.

SaavnAPI.search_albums

Searches for albums.

SaavnAPI.search_artists

Searches for artists.

SaavnAPI.search_playlists

Searches for playlists.

SaavnAPI.search_songs

Searches for songs.
