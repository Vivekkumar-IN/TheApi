Methods¶

The following methods are available in SaavnAPI:

SaavnAPI.get_album([album_id, album_link])

Retrieves an album by its ID or link.

SaavnAPI.get_artist(artist_id[, page, ...])

Retrieves an artist by their ID.

SaavnAPI.get_artist_albums(artist_id[, ...])

Retrieves a list of albums for a given artist by their ID.

SaavnAPI.get_artist_songs(artist_id[, page, ...])

Retrieves a list of songs for a given artist by their ID.

SaavnAPI.get_playlist([playlist_id, ...])

Retrieves a playlist by its ID or link.

SaavnAPI.get_song_by_id(song_id)

Retrieves a song by its ID.

SaavnAPI.get_song_lyrics(song_id)

Retrieves lyrics for a song by its ID.

SaavnAPI.get_song_suggestions(song_id[, limit])

Retrieves song suggestions based on the given song ID.

SaavnAPI.search(query)

Searches globally for songs, albums, artists, and playlists.

SaavnAPI.search_albums(query[, page, limit])

Searches for albums.

SaavnAPI.search_artists(query[, page, limit])

Searches for artists.

SaavnAPI.search_playlists(query[, page, limit])

Searches for playlists.

SaavnAPI.search_songs(query[, page, limit])

Searches for songs.
